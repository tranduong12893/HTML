The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of
ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-
invented in 1980's England, when stylized verdions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorousl
maintained English estate gardens

The earliest cottage gardens were more pratical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and
herbs, along tith some fruit trees.
